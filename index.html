Pinsao Proper

Welcome to Barangay Pinsao Proper, a place where tradition meets natural beauty. Explore the unique landscapes of Stone Kingdom, savor local flavors at Farmer's Daughter Restaurant, and enjoy the peaceful views from Tam-awan Village. Here, every corner has a story, and we're excited to share our vibrant community with you!

Our Services

Health Services

Barangay health centers provide free consultations, immunizations, maternal care, and first aid assistance to residents.

Environmental Programs

Regular clean-up drives, waste segregation campaigns, and tree-planting activities to promote a greener community.

Disaster Preparedness

Training and simulations for disaster response, evacuation centers, and emergency relief services during calamities.

Livelihood Assistance

Workshops and programs to empower residents with skills in handicrafts, urban gardening, and small-scale entrepreneurship.

Community Development

Organizing youth sports activities, senior citizen programs, and family-focused community events.

Vision

The most ecologically friendly barangay in the City of Baguio.

Mission

We are committed to preserving the environment by planting trees, conserving water resources, and intensifying practices in organic backyard gardening to ensure the health of future generations and to attain Zero Waste Management community-wide.

Core Values

We are always available in extending our quality service with dedication and teamwork toward excellence.
